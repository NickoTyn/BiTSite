About us

Founded five years ago, the BIT Association is an integral part of the Faculty of Engineering in Sibiu. Initially created to support engineering students from the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology , our association includes profiles such as ISM (Multimedia Systems Engineering), TI (Information Technology), and C (Computers).

Our
Mission

BIT aims to bring students together and strengthen the bonds between them and their professors. We offer vast opportunities for personal and professional development, facilitating connections between students and companies eager to be part of the community of their future employees.

Our
Goals

Our primary goal is to bridge the gap between students, the faculty, and the professional world. We provide a platform where students can learn, innovate, and grow. Through close collaboration with top companies, we ensure a smooth transition from student life to professional careers.
