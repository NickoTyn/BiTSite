Exciting opportunity for all students at the Faculty of Engineering in Sibiu!

In partnership with the association BIT, NXP Semiconductors is offering an incredible chance for 10 lucky students to receive financial support for an entire year! The goal? To help passionate learners focus on their studies and grow their knowledge without financial worry.

Who can apply?

Any student from the Faculty of Engineering, no matter the department or year of study! Whether you’re in your first year or about to graduate, this opportunity is for YOU!

Key requirement:

To qualify, you must not be employed or working in any form, including internships. A declaration stating this will be required. If you’re volunteering, you’re welcome to apply—just make sure you aren’t holding a job anywhere else.

This is your chance to boost your learning, dive deeper into your passions, and have the support you need to make the most of your studies!
